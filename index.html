ELECTRONIC & TELECOMMUNICATION ENGINEERING

The department of Electronics and Telecommunication engineering was started in 2009, with an annual intake of 60 students. The department is equipped with state of the art computing facilities and experienced staff member & is known for its academic excellence. The department has Vision and Mission stated below.


Vision


To provide valuable resources for industry and society through excellence in technical education and research.


Mission


  1. Educating students with requisite technical expertise to meet the growing challenges of the industry .
  2. Promoting research through constant interaction with research bodies and various industries .
  3. Equipping students with fundamental subject knowledge to enable them for continuing education.
